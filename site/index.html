Shane Curry

Shane Curry is a professional animator with 10 years experience in primetime television and streaming. His independent cartoons span 2D and 3D, including extensive animation/rigging/systems building in Blender, Unreal Engine, ToonBoom Harmony, Unity, Godot, Roblox, and most recently, vanilla JavaScript and C (with the help of AI). He is currently building his own Vector Based Animation Software.
