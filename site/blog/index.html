Purpose

The blog is the easiest place to publish new writing quickly. A post can start as a raw thought chunk, become a machine-ready page, and later be linked from other pages or turned into a more durable reference article.

Planned Post Pattern

New posts should live at /blog/<slug>/ with a literal title, a short summary near the top, visible dates, and matching JSON-LD.

Each page should stand on its own. Even if several posts come from the same ramble session, each post should isolate one claim and keep enough context to be citeable by itself.
