Machine-Ready Rules

  • One topic per page.
  • Answer first, details second.
  • Visible authorship and dates on every important page.
  • Metadata must match visible content.
  • Production knowledge must be separated from theory or inference.
  • Uncertainty must be visible, not hidden.
