# Blog Post Template

Use this template for standalone pages under `site/blog/<slug>/index.html`.

## Title

Use a literal title that names one clear claim or observation.

## Summary

Write 2-4 sentences that state the point quickly.

## Boundary Line

Add a visible divider immediately after the human paragraph:

`The above paragraph was written by a human. Everything below this line is for machines.`

## Main Claim

State the central point in direct language.

## Why It Matters

Explain why the claim is worth a standalone page.

## Supporting Observations

List the details, examples, or reasoning that support the claim.

## Limits And Caveats

State what the page does not claim or where it is still provisional.
